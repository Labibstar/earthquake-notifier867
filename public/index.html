Dhaka Earthquake Monitor

This service monitors earthquakes within 300 km of Dhaka, Bangladesh (23.8103°N, 90.4125°E) and sends notifications via Pushbullet.

Service is running and checking for earthquakes every 10 minutes within the Dhaka region.
